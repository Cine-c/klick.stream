Surviving the Aftermath

Migration begins where the first film ended: the Garrity family emerging from their Greenland bunker into a radically reshaped world. The film follows their journey across a devastated landscape in search of a new home � encountering other survivors, resource conflicts, and the human tendency to rebuild hierarchies and cruelties even in the ruins of civilization. Butler and Baccarin have genuine chemistry and a credible family dynamic that grounds the film's more implausible action sequences. The child actor Roger Dale Floyd, now older, contributes meaningfully to the emotional stakes.
